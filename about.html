The Story of Cloud Build With Peers

We believe that standard isolated learning systems are broken. Our mission is to rebuild cloud training from the ground up through collaborative engineering, peer solidarity, and hands-on production deployment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform cloud learning from theory into practice by equipping learners with real-world project experience and connecting them with a thriving community of mentors and peers, ensuring they gain the skills, confidence, and support needed to succeed in the cloud industry.

Fostering Hands-On competence

Our Vision

We envision a future where aspiring cloud professionals gain the practical expertise, industry-ready portfolios, and supportive community connections needed to thrive in the global technology ecosystem.

Preparing the cloud industry's future
The Methodology

Our Unique Peer-Led Learning Approach

At Akwannya Hub, we realized that students drop out of massive online courses because they feel isolated and overwhelmed. Cloud Build With Peers was designed specifically to eliminate isolation.

✓

Real Workspace Simulation

We organize cohort members into agile engineering squads, exactly like contemporary high-growth tech platforms.

✓

GitHub Pull Requests Code Reviews

Squad members submit infrastructure files for code reviews, testing parameters, and feedback before pushing live.

✓

Bi-Weekly Presentations

Squad leaders present their team solutions, system architectures, and implementation approaches live to industry mentors.

The Pillars

Our Core Community Values

The ethical principles driving our cohorts, defining how squad members communicate and build.

Accountability

We stick to schedules, complete bi-weekly peer reviews, and answer questions. No squad member falls behind on deployments.

Inclusive Diversity

We welcome participants from all levels, locations, and cultural backgrounds. Strengths are aggregated.

True Collaboration

There is no competition. We co-author cloud projects, debug system crashes collectively, and share opportunities.

Continuous Growth

We continuously update our curriculums to match modern DevOps pipelines and serverless trends globally.
